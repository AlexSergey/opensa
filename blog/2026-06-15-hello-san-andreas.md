# Hello, San Andreas

_2026-06-15_

OpenSA is an open-source, from-scratch re-creation of the RenderWare engine that runs **GTA San Andreas
in the browser** — the real world, models, collision and physics, with no install.

This is the first post. The plan is to use this blog for short dev notes: what was built, what broke, and
how the engine port is coming along. It lives in the repo (`/blog`) for now and may move to a proper site
later.

More soon.
